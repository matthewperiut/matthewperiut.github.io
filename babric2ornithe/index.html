babric2ornithe

v0.1.0

Migrate Babric-based Minecraft mod projects to Ornithe. Handles mapping migration, build.gradle transformation, dependency management, and more.

macOS / Linux

$ curl -fsSL https://matthewperiut.github.io/babric2ornithe/install.sh | sh

Windows (PowerShell)

> irm https://matthewperiut.github.io/babric2ornithe/install.ps1 | iex
